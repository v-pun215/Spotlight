About Spotlight

Uncover Delhi's hidden gems.

Spotlight is your guide to exploring local treasures – from historic landmarks to buzzing events – right at your fingertips. We bring together real-time data, ratings, and crowd insights to help you discover places that deserve your attention.

Whether you're new to the city or rediscovering your hometown, Spotlight uses location-based recommendations, Wikipedia summaries, and Foursquare data to make your experience smarter and more personal.

Built with ❤️ using open APIs, Spotlight is a passion project focused on exploration, simplicity, and local stories.
